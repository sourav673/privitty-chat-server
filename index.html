Gmail Setup Guide for Privitty Chat

Step 1: Enable 2-Step Verification

Go to your Google Account and enable 2-Step Verification.

Step 2: Generate an App-Specific Password

Visit Google's App Passwords Page and follow the instructions to generate a password.

Step 3: Log in to Privitty Chat
  • Create a new profile.
  • Enter your name.
  • Select Use Other Server.
  • Choose Classic Email Login.
  • Enter your Gmail ID and the App password.
  • Click ✅ to proceed.
Step 4: Enable IMAP (If You Face Issues)
  • Go to ⚙️ Gmail Settings.
  • Click See all settings.
  • Navigate to Forwarding and POP/IMAP.
  • Find IMAP access and select Enable IMAP.
